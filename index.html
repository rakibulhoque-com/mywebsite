About Me

I'm a Senior Data Engineer and AI Platform builder with over years of professional experience. Currently embedded as a forward-deployed engineer at G-Star Raw, where I own the full lifecycle of a production-grade, cloud-native data and AI platform on Google Cloud Platform.

My work at G-Star spans platform engineering, data warehouse architecture, analytics engineering, sustainability reporting, and applied AI product development — across development, UAT, and production environments. The platform I built serves 300+ business users globally through an AI-powered natural-language analytics assistant I designed and delivered end-to-end.

Before G-Star, I co-architected data engineering and ML platforms at Pathao Limited (Bangladesh's leading super-app), consulted on data infrastructure at 10MinuteSchool and Buildnow Inc., and published research on deep learning for digital forensics at BUET, where I earned my degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering.

I'm passionate about building systems that work at production scale — clean architecture, multi-layer security, full audit traceability, and real business impact.
